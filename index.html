    What's to know?

    Hi, I go by Sonia Frances. I am currently a student at Randolph Community College majoring in Information Technology (c/o 2024). My significant other and I own a small restaurant in Asheboro called Gather. We sell the freshest most tastiest Caribbean Asian Fusion Soul cuisine in all of North Carolina. I am responsible for ads, marketing and growing our social media presence on Instagram and Facebook. On a regular basis I am most likely in the kitchen rolling sushi or frying chicken. I enjoy creating applications, roller-skating, and eating exotic food. I want to eventually travel the world while also helping others in need. I believe in racial exclusivity in the tech industry and will continue to pass on the knowledge I’ve received (and continue to receive) to the masses.
